# Privacy Policy

**Last updated**: [DATE]

**ORDERS App** ("we", "our", or "us") operates the ORDERS App mobile application (the "Service").

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information
When you register for ORDERS App, we collect:
- **Name**: First and last name
- **Phone Number**: For authentication and communication
- **WhatsApp Number**: For order communication
- **Email Address**: For account management
- **Identity Document**: ID card photo (for drivers only, for verification purposes)
- **Vehicle Information**: For drivers (moto/vespa details)

### 1.2 Location Information
We collect and process location data when you use our Service:
- **Real-time location**: To match clients with nearby merchants and drivers
- **Background location**: For drivers, to update delivery status and provide accurate ETAs
- **GPS coordinates**: Stored with user profiles for service functionality

### 1.3 Photos and Media
- **Product Photos**: Merchants can upload photos of their products
- **Profile Photos**: Users can upload profile pictures
- **Identity Documents**: Drivers upload ID cards for verification

### 1.4 Usage Information
- Orders placed, delivered, or fulfilled
- Messages exchanged between users
- Payment transactions
- App usage statistics

### 1.5 Device Information
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- Mobile network information

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

### 2.1 Service Delivery
- Creating and managing your account
- Processing orders and payments
- Matching clients with merchants and drivers
- Facilitating communication between users
- Tracking deliveries in real-time
- Verifying driver identity and eligibility

### 2.2 Payment Processing
- Processing payments via Orange Money and Paystack
- Calculating and distributing driver earnings (90/10 split)
- Processing daily payouts to drivers
- Managing merchant sales and payouts

### 2.3 Service Improvement
- Analyzing usage patterns
- Improving app functionality
- Providing customer support
- Preventing fraud and abuse

### 2.4 Communication
- Sending order notifications
- Providing delivery updates
- Sending promotional offers (with your consent)
- Responding to your inquiries

## 3. How We Share Your Information

We share your information only in the following circumstances:

### 3.1 With Other Users
- **Clients** can see merchant names, locations, and product information
- **Drivers** can see client delivery addresses and order details when they accept an order
- **Merchants** can see order details and client contact information for fulfillment
- **All parties** in an order can see each other's names and contact information to facilitate delivery

### 3.2 With Service Providers
- **Supabase**: Database and authentication services (data hosting)
- **Orange Money**: Payment processing for local transactions
- **Paystack**: Credit card payment processing
- **Cloud Storage**: For storing photos and media

### 3.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

### 3.4 Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity.

## 4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data:
- Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure authentication using Supabase Auth
- Row Level Security (RLS) policies in our database
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and authentication

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to:
- Provide our services
- Comply with legal obligations
- Resolve disputes
- Enforce our agreements

When you delete your account:
- Your personal information is removed from active databases
- Order history may be retained for legal and accounting purposes
- Anonymized data may be retained for analytics

## 7. Location Data

### 7.1 Why We Collect Location
- Match clients with nearby merchants and drivers
- Calculate delivery distances and fees
- Provide real-time delivery tracking
- Show merchant locations to clients

### 7.2 How to Control Location Access
- **iOS**: Settings > Privacy > Location Services > ORDERS App
- **Android**: Settings > Apps > ORDERS App > Permissions > Location

**Note**: Disabling location services will prevent you from using core features of the app.

## Instructions for Deploying This Privacy Policy

1. **Customize the template**:
   - Replace [DATE] with today's date
   - Replace [YOUR_SUPPORT_EMAIL] with your support email
   - Replace [YOUR_BUSINESS_ADDRESS] with your business address
   - Replace [YOUR_SUPPORT_PHONE] with your support phone

2. **Host the policy**:
   - Option 1: Create a simple website (GitHub Pages is free)
   - Option 2: Add to your existing website
   - Option 3: Use a privacy policy generator service

3. **Get the URL**:
   - The URL will be something like: `https://yourdomain.com/privacy-policy`

4. **Add to app stores**:
   - Google Play: Add URL in "Store listing" → "Privacy policy"
   - Apple App Store: Add URL in "App Information" → "Privacy Policy URL"

5. **Add to app**:
   - Link to privacy policy in your app's settings or registration screens

6. **Legal review**:
   - Have a lawyer review your privacy policy before publishing
   - Ensure compliance with local laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
