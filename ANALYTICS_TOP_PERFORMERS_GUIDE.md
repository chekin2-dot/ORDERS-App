# Analytics Top Performers Guide

## Overview

The Analytics & Performance screen now includes comprehensive revenue tracking per merchant, driver, and client, showing which actors are boosting the business. This feature helps identify top performers and understand revenue distribution across the platform.

## Key Features

### 1. Top Merchants (Boosting Business)

**What it shows**:
- Top 5 merchants by revenue for the selected period
- Shop name
- Contact phone number
- Total revenue generated
- Number of completed orders

**Ranking System**:
- #1 merchant gets a gold award badge
- #2-5 get numbered rankings
- Sorted by revenue (highest first)

**Business Insights**:
- Identify best-performing merchants
- Understand revenue concentration
- Target high performers for partnership programs
- Recognize and reward top sellers

### 2. Top Drivers (Boosting Business)

**What it shows**:
- Top 5 drivers by earnings for the selected period
- Driver full name
- Contact phone number
- Total earnings from deliveries
- Number of completed deliveries

**Ranking System**:
- #1 driver gets a gold award badge
- #2-5 get numbered rankings
- Sorted by earnings (highest first)

**Business Insights**:
- Identify most active drivers
- Understand delivery capacity
- Target top drivers for incentive programs
- Recognize high-performing delivery partners

### 3. Top Clients (Boosting Business)

**What it shows**:
- Top 5 clients by spending for the selected period
- Client full name
- Contact phone number
- Total spending on orders
- Number of completed orders

**Ranking System**:
- #1 client gets a gold award badge
- #2-5 get numbered rankings
- Sorted by spending (highest first)

**Business Insights**:
- Identify highest-value customers
- Understand customer loyalty
- Target VIP clients for retention programs
- Recognize and reward best customers

## Period Selection

All top performer data adjusts based on the selected period:

### Day View
- Shows top performers for the last 24 hours
- Real-time identification of daily stars
- Useful for daily operations and instant recognition

### Week View
- Shows top performers for the last 7 days
- Weekly performance tracking
- Ideal for short-term trend analysis

### Month View (Default)
- Shows top performers for the last 30 days
- Monthly performance overview
- Best for strategic planning and rewards

## Visual Elements

### Performance Cards

Each performer card displays:
- **Rank Badge**: Gold award for #1, number for others
- **Name**: Full name or shop name
- **Phone**: Contact information
- **Revenue/Earnings**: In F CFA, color-coded green
- **Orders/Deliveries**: Total count
- **Type Icon**: Store (merchant), Truck (driver), Users (client)

### Color Coding

- **Merchants**: Orange theme (#f59e0b)
- **Drivers**: Green theme (#10b981)
- **Clients**: Blue theme (#3b82f6)

### Award Badge

The #1 position in each category features a special gold award icon, highlighting the top performer.

## Business Intelligence

### Revenue Concentration Analysis

**Key Metrics**:

1. **Merchant Revenue Share**
   - What % of total revenue comes from top 5 merchants?
   - High concentration (>50%): Consider diversification
   - Low concentration (<20%): Well-distributed business

2. **Client Spending Share**
   - What % of total revenue comes from top 5 clients?
   - High concentration: VIP retention critical
   - Low concentration: Broad customer base

3. **Driver Capacity**
   - Total deliveries from top 5 drivers
   - Indicates delivery dependency
   - Helps with capacity planning

## Interpreting Results

### Healthy Distribution

**Good Signs**:
- Top 5 < 40% of total revenue
- Diverse merchant base
- Multiple active drivers
- Broad customer base

**Actions**:
- Maintain balance
- Support all tiers
- Encourage growth
- Monitor trends

### Concentration Risk

**Warning Signs**:
- Top 5 > 60% of total revenue
- Single merchant dominance
- Few active drivers
- Limited customer base

**Actions**:
- Recruit more merchants
- Onboard more drivers
- Expand marketing
- Diversify offerings

## Troubleshooting

### No Data Showing

**Possible Causes**:
- No completed orders in period
- Selected period too short
- Database connection issue

**Solutions**:
- Try longer period (month)
- Verify orders exist
- Check internet connection
- Refresh the screen

### Rankings Seem Wrong

**Possible Causes**:
- Only counts delivered orders
- Period selection affects ranking
- Recent orders not yet completed

**Solutions**:
- Verify order status
- Check period selection
- Wait for order completion
- Refresh data

### Export Missing Sheets

**Possible Causes**:
- No top performers for period
- Less than 5 performers
- Data loading incomplete

**Solutions**:
- Try longer period
- Verify completed orders
- Wait for data to load
- Try export again
